## Base

## Modifiers

### Sizes

Three sizes available: 'sm', 'md' and 'lg':

<Canvas of={Stories.Sizes} />

### Items status

Four statuses available:

- **Completed**: default `stepper__item` modifier and `badge--sucess`

<Canvas of={Stories.StatusCompleted} />

- **Current**: `stepper__item--active` modifier and `badge--secondary badge--is-inverse`

<Canvas of={Stories.StatusCurrent} />

- **Pending**: default `stepper__item` modifier and `badge--neutral`

<Canvas of={Stories.StatusPending} />

- **Disabled**: `stepper__item--disabled` modifier and `badge--neutral`

<Canvas of={Stories.StatusDisabled} />
